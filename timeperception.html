Description

A study on loading screens' animation speed and their effect on user's time perception. Results suggest that with a faster animation, perceived wait time gets shorter and a generally more satisfied user.

Method

Theoretical research was conducted before creating and testing three almost identical applications, the only difference between them was the animation speed for the loading screens. A test was then conducted on 24 participants, letting them load up each application with a follow-up survey.
